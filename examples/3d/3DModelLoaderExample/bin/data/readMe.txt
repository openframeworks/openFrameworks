Black Footed Penguin / African Penguin
Spheniscus demersus


The African penguin (Spheniscus demersus), also known as the jackass penguin and black-footed penguin is a species ofpenguin, confined to southern African waters. It is also widely known as the "jackass" penguin for its donkey-like bray.
